Recent Projects

  • Outern is a platform that will give companies and students an entirely new way to interact in a work setting. Companies are able to offer students "Micro Internships" remotely, with the goal of building working relationships with said students that may eventually lead to an internship of a full-time hire. This application is being built using ReactJS, Google Cloud Firestore, and various Third-Party API's such as Stripe and Algolia.
  • Python based stock trading, using Alpaca's trading API. A work in progress, with the goal of automating the trading process fully.
